# JEDI FUGITIVE: ECHOES OF THE FALLEN
## Game Summary & What to Expect

---

## üìñ Story

You are a Jedi Padawan on a covert reconnaissance mission to a remote Sith-controlled world. Your ship has been shot down by orbital defenses, crash-landing in hostile territory surrounded by ancient Sith tombs and ruins.

**The Situation:**
- Your comms are dead - no way to call for help
- Sith forces patrol the crash site  
- Ancient tombs dot the landscape, pulsing with dark energy
- The Sith have stolen 3 precious Jedi artifacts and hidden them deep within their most dangerous tombs, using them as focal points to corrupt the planet

**Your Mission:**
Infiltrate the Sith tombs, recover the 3 stolen Jedi artifacts, and use them to power your ship's communications array. But there's a catch - the artifacts have been corrupted by dark side rituals. The deeper you delve into the tombs, the more the darkness will test your resolve.

**The Question:**
Will you remain true to the Jedi Code, or will the seductive power of the Dark Side claim another fallen apprentice?

---

## üéÆ Gameplay Loop

### Phase 1: Survival & Exploration (Early Game)
- Navigate the hostile crash site biome
- Fight or avoid Sith patrols and wildlife
- Scavenge for weapons, armor, and supplies
- Discover Points of Interest (POIs) that reveal lore and grant Force powers
- Level up your character and equipment

### Phase 2: Tomb Raiding (Mid Game)
- Find and enter Sith Tombs (marked 'D' on map)
- Descend 3-5 levels deep into each tomb
- Fight increasingly powerful Sith enemies:
  - **Level 1-2:** Sith Troopers (cannon fodder)
  - **Level 3-4:** Sith Acolytes (Force-sensitive cultists)
  - **Level 5+:** Sith Warriors (dark Jedi)
- Reach the deepest chamber and claim the corrupted Jedi artifact
- Escape the tomb alive
- **Goal:** Recover artifacts from 3 different tombs

### Phase 3: Extraction & Final Confrontation (End Game)
- Return to crash site with 3 artifacts
- Place artifacts in the comms terminal to power it up
- Transmit distress beacon to the Jedi Council
- **THE TWIST:** Your alignment determines who answers...
  - **Light Side Path:** A Sith Lord arrives to stop you - you must defeat them to escape
  - **Dark Side Path:** The Jedi Council senses your corruption - a Jedi Master arrives to eliminate you
- Defeat the final boss and board your ship
- Receive judgment from the Jedi Council based on your corruption level

## ‚öñÔ∏è The Corruption System

**Every choice matters.** Your actions throughout the game affect your Dark Side corruption (0-100):

### How Corruption Increases:
- Using Dark Side Force powers (Choke, Drain, Fear, Lightning)
- Killing enemies ruthlessly
- Embracing anger and aggression in combat
- Interacting with Sith artifacts and altars
- Reading too deeply into Sith lore

### How to Resist Corruption:
- Using Light Side powers (Heal, Protect, Meditation)
- Showing mercy and restraint
- Following the Jedi Code
- Meditating to cleanse yourself
- Quick, efficient escapes over prolonged carnage

## üéØ Victory Conditions

### To Win the Game:
1. **Survive** the crash site and reach at least level 10-12
2. **Infiltrate** 3 Sith tombs and recover all 3 Jedi artifacts
3. **Power** the comms terminal with the artifacts
4. **Defeat** the alignment-based final boss who arrives
5. **Board** your ship to trigger the ending sequence

- **Average Time:** ~190 turns (~2-3 hours)
- **Average Level:** 11-12

- **Most Common Death:** Tomb combat (73% of failures)

**Verdict:** Victory is achievable through skill, strategy, and preparation!

## üí° Strategic Tips

### For Light Side Victory (Recommended for First Playthrough):
- **Prioritize:** HP, Defense, and healing items
- **Force Powers:** Heal, Protect, Meditation (free Force restoration)
- **Combat:** Defensive, tactical, minimize Force power usage
- **Exploration:** Thorough - find all items and POIs for max power
- **Tombs:** Go slow, heal between fights, escape if needed
- **Result:** Stronger powers when aligned, heroic ending

### For Dark Side Victory (Hard Mode):
- **Prioritize:** Attack, Force energy, aggressive combat
- **Force Powers:** Choke, Drain, Lightning (lifesteal sustain)
- **Combat:** Aggressive, use Force liberally, end fights fast
- **Exploration:** Fast and brutal - efficiency over thoroughness
- **Tombs:** Blitz through, use dark powers to dominate
- **Result:** Powerful but morally bankrupt, tragic ending

### Universal Survival Tips:
- **Healing is key:** Stock up on medkits and food
- **Level matters:** Don't rush tombs at low level
- **Force regeneration:** +20/turn peaceful, +5/turn in combat
- **Equipment:** Upgrade weapons and armor whenever possible
- **Meditation:** Use it! Free Force restoration out of combat
- **POIs:** Explore every ? ! @ symbol - they grant Force powers
- **Boss prep:** Full HP, full Force, best gear before final fight

## üîÆ Force Powers

### Light Side Powers:
- **Force Heal:** Restore HP (scales with mastery)
- **Force Protect:** Temporary defense buff
- **Force Meditation:** Restore Force + reduce stress (out of combat only)
- **Force Reveal:** Detect hidden areas
- **Force Push/Pull:** Reposition enemies

### Dark Side Powers:
- **Force Choke:** High damage + stun
- **Force Drain:** Damage enemy, heal yourself
- **Force Fear:** Area effect - all enemies flee
- **Force Lightning:** Raw damage

### Power Scaling:
- Powers scale with your alignment mastery (0-3 levels)
- Using aligned powers = cheaper cost, more damage
- Using opposing powers = 2x cost, 50% effectiveness
- Choose your path wisely!

## üåç What to Expect: The World

### Crash Site Biome:
- Open wasteland with scattered ruins
- Your crashed ship (S) - the extraction point
- Comms terminal (C) - needs 3 artifacts to activate
- Sith patrol camps with enemies
- Ancient altars and shrines (POIs)
- 3-5 Tomb entrances (D) scattered across the map

### Inside Sith Tombs:
- 3-5 procedurally generated dungeon levels
- Darker, tighter corridors (reduced vision)
- Enemies spawn in rooms and corridors
- Loot: weapons, armor, healing items, materials
- **Final chamber:** Contains 1 corrupted Jedi artifact
- Stairs up (<) and down (>)
- Can exit anytime by going back up

### Enemies You'll Face:
- **Sith Troopers:** Basic soldiers, ranged blasters
- **Sith Acolytes:** Force users, dark powers
- **Sith Warriors:** Elite dark Jedi, dangerous
- **Sith Ghosts:** Undead spirits haunting tombs
- **Final Boss - Sith Lord:** If you stay Light, face a powerful Sith
- **Final Boss - Jedi Master:** If you go Dark, face your former allies

## üìä Progression Systems

### Character Leveling:
- Gain XP from combat
- Level up ‚Üí +HP, +Attack, +Defense
- Max practical level: ~12-15

### Equipment:
- **Weapons:** Vibroblade, Blaster, Lightsaber (rare)
- **Armor:** Energy Shields, Combat Armor, Robes
- **Dual-wielding:** Equip weapon in offhand for extra damage

### Force Energy:
- Capacity: 100 (replaces limited Force Points)
- Regeneration: +20/turn peaceful, +5/turn combat
- Powers cost 0-25 Force
- Energy bar shown in UI

### Alignment Mastery:
- Mastery Level 0-3 based on corruption commitment
- Higher mastery = stronger aligned powers
- Pure Light (0-20) or Pure Dark (80-100) = Max mastery
