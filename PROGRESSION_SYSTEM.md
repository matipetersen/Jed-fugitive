# Jedi Fugitive - Complete Progression System

## Dual Path Leveling System

The game features a **dual-path leveling system** where players can progress on both Light Side and Dark Side paths simultaneously. Your effective level is the **higher** of the two paths.

### Base Level Progression

**Every Level Up (Both Paths):**
- +5 Max HP
- +1 Attack
- +1 Defense  
- +1 Evasion
- -30 Stress (major stress reduction)
- +1 Force Point (every 3 levels)

**Level Up Choice Menu:**
When leveling up, you can choose ONE additional bonus:
1. +5 Max HP (extra)
2. +1 Attack (extra)
3. +1 Evasion (extra)
4. Learn a Force Ability

### XP Requirements

**Per Level:**
- Next Level XP = Current Level × 100
- Example: Level 1→2 needs 100 XP, Level 2→3 needs 200 XP, etc.

**XP Sources:**
- **Killing Enemies**: 10-50 XP based on enemy tier
- **Absorbing Artifacts**: Dark Side XP + corruption
- **Destroying Artifacts**: Light Side XP + purity
- **Completing Objectives**: Variable XP

## Dark Side Path Progression

### Level Unlocks & Force Powers

| Level | Base Stats | Force Ability | Description |
|-------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| **1** | Starting stats | Force Push | Basic telekinetic ability |
| **2** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Force Choke** | Crush an enemy's throat from afar |
| **3** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Force Lightning** | Unleash devastating lightning |
| **4** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Drain Life** | Steal HP from enemies |
| **5** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Dark Rage** | Boost attack but lose control |
| **6** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Dominate Mind** | Control weak-willed enemies |
| **7+** | Continue +5 HP, +1 all | More abilities available | Path-specific powers |

### Dark Side Mechanics

**Corruption System:**
- Starts at 0%, increases to 100%
- **Absorbing artifacts**: +10-20% corruption
- **Killing without mercy**: +1-3% corruption
- **Using dark powers**: +2-5% corruption per use

**Corruption Effects:**

| Corruption | Effects |
|------------|---------|
| 0-25% | **Temptation**: Minor stress when resisting dark urges |
| 26-50% | **Tainted**: -5 Defense, +5 Attack, dark powers cost less Force |
| 51-75% | **Corrupted**: -10 Defense, +10 Attack, enemies fear you, harder to use light powers |
| 76-100% | **Dark Lord**: -15 Defense, +15 Attack, permanent fear aura, light powers unavailable |

**Benefits:**
- Higher damage output (+15 Attack at 100%)
- Fear effects on weaker enemies
- Reduced Force costs for dark abilities
- Enemies may flee or submit

**Drawbacks:**
- Lower defense (-15 Defense at 100%)
- Cannot purify without redemption
- Alignment-locked dialogue options
- Harder to maintain alliances

| **1** | Starting stats | Force Push | Basic telekinetic ability |
| **2** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Force Heal** | Restore HP with the Force |
| **3** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Force Shield** | Create a protective barrier |
| **4** | +5 HP, +1 Atk, +1 Def, +1 Eva | **Battle Meditation** | Boost all stats temporarily |

| **7+** | Continue +5 HP, +1 all | More abilities available | Path-specific powers |

### Light Side Mechanics

**Purity System:**
- Corruption decreases, purity increases
- **Destroying artifacts**: -10-20% corruption
- **Mercy/sparing enemies**: -2-5% corruption
- **Using light powers**: Maintains low corruption

**Purity Effects:**

| Corruption | Benefits |
|------------|----------|
| 0-10% | **Pure Light**: +10 Defense, +5 Evasion, meditation heals 20-30% HP |
| 11-25% | **Light Aligned**: +5 Defense, +3 Evasion, meditation heals 15-25% HP |
| 26-50% | **Balanced**: Standard stats, meditation heals 10-20% HP |
| 51-75% | **Darkening**: -3 Defense, meditation heals 5-10% HP |
| 76-100% | **Fallen**: See Dark Side effects |

**Benefits:**
- Higher defense (+10 Defense at 0% corruption)
- Better evasion (+5 Evasion at pure)
- Stronger healing abilities
- Can redeem corruption
- Allies more likely to help

**Drawbacks:**
- Lower raw damage output
- Must resist dark temptations
- Cannot use most dark powers
- Slower corruption recovery

### Crafting Mechanics

**How to Craft:**
1. Press `C` at any time to open crafting menu
2. Select recipe from available list
3. Confirm to consume materials
4. Item/upgrade applied instantly

**Skill System:**
- Craft Level starts at 0
- Each successful craft gives +1 skill XP
- Skill Level = Total Crafts ÷ 10
- Higher recipes require higher skill

**Material Sources:**
- Enemy drops (15% chance)
- Loot chests in tombs
- Trade with NPCs (rare)
- Scavenging crash site debris

## Optimal Build Strategies

### Glass Cannon (Pure Dark)
**Goal**: Maximum damage, accept fragility
- Focus all level choices on Attack
- Absorb every artifact (max corruption fast)
- Craft weapon upgrades (Kyber Attunement end-game)
- Use Dark Rage in tough fights
- **Stats at Level 10**: 30 Attack, 3 Defense, 95 HP

### Tank (Pure Light)
**Goal**: Maximum survival, steady damage
- Focus level choices on HP and Evasion
- Destroy every artifact (stay pure)
- Craft Beskar armor upgrades
- Use Force Shield + Battle Meditation
- **Stats at Level 10**: 23 Defense, 18 Evasion, 120 HP

### Balanced Hybrid
**Goal**: Flexibility, access to both power sets
- Mix absorb/destroy artifacts (maintain 40-60% corruption)
- Take varied level bonuses
- Learn both power types
- Craft balanced upgrades
- **Stats at Level 10**: 15 Attack, 13 Defense, 95 HP

### Speedrunner
**Goal**: Rush objectives, minimal combat
- Focus Evasion for fleeing
- Craft movement items
- Use Force Enlightenment to find objectives
- Minimize fights, maximize XP efficiency
- **Target**: Complete in <1,500 turns

## Tips & Strategies

### General Advice
- **Early Game**: Focus on survival (HP, Defense)
- **Mid Game**: Decide your alignment path (commit to one)
- **Late Game**: Max out chosen path, craft legendary items
- **Meditation**: Use regularly but watch for enemy spawns!
- **Stress**: Keep below 70 to avoid penalties

### Combat Tips
- **Dark Side**: Burst damage, kill before they hit you
- **Light Side**: Sustain, heal between fights
- **Hybrid**: Adapt to situation, use appropriate powers

### Crafting Priority
1. **Early**: Medkits (survival)
2. **Mid**: Weapon sharpening (clear faster)
3. **Late**: Legendary upgrades (Beskar/Kyber)

### Artifact Strategy
- **Pure Dark**: Absorb all, rush to 100% corruption for massive Attack
- **Pure Light**: Destroy all, stay at 0% for maximum Defense
- **Hybrid**: Alternate based on current needs
