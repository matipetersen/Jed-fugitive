COMBAT TIPS:

  GENERAL STRATEGY:
  • Learn enemy symbols and behaviors
  • Adapt tactics based on enemy composition
  • Use the environment to your advantage
  • Manage resources carefully

  TARGETING SYSTEMS:
  • Force Abilities (f): Variable range, powerful effects
  • Grenades (t): 3-tile range, area damage
  • Guns (F): Weapon-dependent range, accurate damage

  PRIORITY TARGETS:
  1. Snipers (if you can close distance)
  2. Assassins (if they're isolated)
  3. Berserkers (before they reach you)
  4. Troopers (balanced threat)
  5. Guardians (last - too tanky)
